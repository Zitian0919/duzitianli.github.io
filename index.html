About Me

Hi, I'm Duzitian Li, 李杜子添, a Master's student studying atmospheric aerosols at Nanjing University. My research combines mass spectrometric measurements with kinetic chemical reactions to investigate the formation of iodic acid (HIO3) as an aerosol source and its contribution to nanoparticle growth. My analysis utilizes both ambient datasets and chamber experiment results.
